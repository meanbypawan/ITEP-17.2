SQLAlchemy Practice Exercise: Library Management System
Problem Statement

Create a REST-ready SQLAlchemy application for managing a Library System.

The system has two entities:

Author
Book

Relationship:

Author 1 -------- * Book

One Author can have many Books
One Book belongs to one Author
