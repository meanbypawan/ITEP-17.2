SQLAlchemy Product CRUD & Query Operations Assignment
Model Information

You have a SQLAlchemy model named Product.

Product Table
Field	Type
id	Integer (Primary Key)
title	String
price	Float
discount_percentage	Float
brand	String
category_name	String

Example data:

id	title	        price	discount_percentage	brand	category_name
1	iPhone 15	80000	10	                Apple	Mobile
2	Galaxy S24	70000	15	                Samsung	Mobile
3	MacBook Air	120000	5	                Apple	Laptop
4	ThinkPad X1	150000	20	                Lenovo	Laptop
5	Dell Inspiron	60000	12	                Dell	Laptop
Part A: Basic CRUD Operations
Question 1: Create Product

Create a function create_product() which accepts product details and inserts a new product into the database.

Example:

create_product(p:Product)

Product created successfully

2 Galaxy S24 Samsung Mobile
...
