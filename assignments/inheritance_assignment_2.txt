1. Employee Bonus Calculator
Problem Statement

A company gives bonuses differently based on employee type.

Create:

abstract class Employee
child classes:
Developer
Manager
Intern

Rules:

Developer → 20% bonus
Manager → 35% bonus
Intern → fixed ₹5000 bonus
Requirements

Methods:

calculate_bonus()
display_details()
Concepts
Abstract class
Method overriding
Business logic
